IDEF 2019 14th International Defense Industry Fair

Our President Mr. Recep Tayyip Erdoğan visited our booth at IDEF 2019.

The IDEF 2019 – 14th International Defense Industry Fair, which serves as the international showcase of our defense industry, was held between April 30 and May 3, 2019, at the TÜYAP Fair and Congress Center in Istanbul. We participated as companies representing the defense strength of our country.

Following the opening ceremony of the fair, our President Mr. Recep Tayyip Erdoğan visited our booth and received information about the projects we are conducting and the indigenous national systems we have developed. Our product portfolio, technological capabilities, and export targets were presented to him in detail.

The presence of our President was a great honor and a source of motivation for us. Such visits strengthen our institution’s determination to increase our contributions to the localization and development of the defense industry.

We express our gratitude to our President for his visit and thank all the guests who showed interest in our booth.
