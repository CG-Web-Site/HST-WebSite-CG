Our Production Infrastructure

With over 400 production machines and a modern machinery park, we have the capability for large-scale, high-precision manufacturing:
  • Horizontal Machining Centers:
    Suitable for machining large-scale parts with high precision and wide table dimensions.
  • Okuma Horizontal Machining Centers (MC600H, MX60HB, MX40HA):
    Enables high-precision mass production (630x630 mm table size).
  • Horizontal Machining Centers (Mass Production):
    Provides high-efficiency and precision for serial production.
  • Heavy-Duty Horizontal Machining:
    Ideal for heavy-duty operations with high chip removal capacity and long-term process stability.
  • Vertical Machining Centers:
    Offers precise vertical-axis machining with high-speed spindles.
  • Vertical Drilling & Tapping Machines:
    Used for fast and precise drilling and tapping operations.
  • CNC Turning Lines:
    Delivers accurate and efficient turning operations, compatible with robotic integration.
  • Vertical Lathes:
    Enables precise turning of large-diameter parts in vertical position.
  • High-Speed CNC Lathes:
    Ideal for turning operations requiring high precision.
  • Robotic Automation Systems:
    Enhances quality and efficiency by enabling automated part handling and process repetition on production lines.
